FAVOURABLE CONDITION FOR RICE

    Temperature:

16 deg centigrade to 27 deg centigrade. Annual coverage temperature around 24 deg centigrade is ideal


    Rainfall Needed:

100 to 200 cm is ideal for rice growing. But rainfall during harvest times is harmful.


    Soil:

Rice is grown well in alluvial soil or on the fertile river basins. It is also grown in mixed soil or loamy and clayey soil.


    Land:

Plain lands or gentle slopes are suitable for production of rice. Because stagnancy of water is a must for the cultivation of rice.
